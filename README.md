# RPA & Automation Adoption Survey — Power BI Analysis

## Context
Analysis built for a graduate thesis (Mastère Spécialisé, Grenoble École de Management) 
examining how professionals across industries adopt automation tools like Power Automate, 
UiPath, and low-code platforms.

Sample: n = 41 respondents across multiple countries, roles, and industries.

## Dashboard Pages

### Sample Overview
Geographic distribution (Azure Map), respondent breakdown by role level, 
company size, function, and industry.

### General Awareness & Adoption
- Most-used automation tools
- Adoption frequency among respondents
- Training availability in respondents' companies

### Value & Measurement
- Key automation use cases (clustered bar chart)
- Perceived benefits summary (pivot table + heatmap)

### Enablers & Governance
- Governance vs. benefits correlation (scatter chart)
- Skill sources (how professionals learned automation)
- Governance and access heatmap by department

### Barriers
- Top barriers to automation adoption
- Barriers heatmap segmented by department

### Automation Continuity
- Organisational readiness average perception
- Average perception by industry

## Key Finding
Low-code tools (e.g., Power Automate) show significantly higher adoption 
rates than enterprise-grade platforms, driven by accessibility and 
lower training barriers.
